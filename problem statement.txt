A retail company wants to understand how its sales are performing across different products,
regions, and time periods. The management team needs clear insights to identify top-performing products,
low-performing regions, and overall sales trends.



As a Data Analyst, your task is to analyze the sales dataset, clean
and explore the data, generate meaningful visualizations, and summarize key business insights.
The goal is to help the company make better decisions related to inventory, marketing, and sales strategy.

This project focuses on:

1.Analyzing total sales over time

2.Finding top-selling products and regions

3.Identifying sales trends and patterns

4.Creating simple visualizations for business understanding
